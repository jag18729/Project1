Pro Tips

  • Use Tab for auto-completion in both CMD and Terminal
  • Press Ctrl+C (Windows) or Cmd+C (Mac) to stop running commands
  • Use clear (Mac/Linux) or cls (Windows) to clear screen
  • Arrow keys navigate command history in both systems
  • Response times vary: Network commands take 100ms to 5s
