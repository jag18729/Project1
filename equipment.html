My Camera Gear

Current Setup

I primarily use a mirrorless camera that weighs 400 grams and provides excellent image quality. The battery lasts for approximately 1800 shots per charge.

For lenses, I use a standard 50mm lens for portraits and a wide angle 24mm lens for landscapes.

Photography Tip

The best camera is the one you have with you. Focus on composition and lighting rather than expensive gear. As photographer Ansel Adams said: "The single most important component is twelve inches behind the camera."
