Photography Basics

Composition

The rule of thirds divides your frame into a 3x3 grid. Place important subjects along these lines for better composition.

Camera Settings

Understanding exposure is key. Use aperture around f/2.8 for portraits and f/8 for landscapes. Keep ISO at 100 in bright light for best quality.

Pro Tips

Important: Use a tripod for sharp images in low light

The golden hour provides the best natural lighting. Best times: 6:00 AM or 6:00 PM
